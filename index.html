Style Guide · Module 03

AP vs. Broadcast Style

Know both styles cold — dates, addresses, times, numbers, abbreviations, and script rules.

Key rules (study guide): Broadcast scripts do not split words across lines, split sentences across pages, use symbols, or use abbreviations. AP adds periods: a.m. and p.m. Broadcast writes out "in the morning" / "in the afternoon."
